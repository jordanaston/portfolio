about_me

Throughout my life I’ve always been fascinated by technology and it’s possibilities. In my past years I had a heavy focus in sound engineering and music production while playing in a number of bands and working as a live sound engineer and DJ. I’ve always been influenced by music and discovered my creative side through performance, live production and mixing. I’m now pursuing a career in web development to explore a more technical, yet creative side of myself I’ve always wanted to develop.

I currently work as a technical advisor for a company called Concentrix providing support to software problems for iOS, MacOS and media services. In my previous role, I worked as an audio technician for a company called Select AV which involved the setup and live operations of audio and visual equipment for live events and functions. My skills lay in problem solving and creativity which is why a career in web development is so compelling to me.

I have an associate degree in audio engineering and am currently studying a diploma of information technology at Coder Academy Brisbane. Looking forward to sharpening my skills as a developer and start taking on real world projects as soon as possible!
